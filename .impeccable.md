# Impeccable Design Context: Sanad

This project establishes the visual and UX standards for Sanad, a technical services and digital transformation company.

## Design Context

### Users
The primary users are **SME owners (Small and Medium Enterprises) and Operations Managers** in the MENA region. They are looking for reliable digital transformation services to streamline their business workflows. They value professional expertise, clear communication, and efficient results.

### Brand Personality
The Sanad brand is defined by three core pillars:
1. **Reliable**: Building trust (reflecting the meaning of "Sanad" or Support).
2. **Empowering**: Providing the tools and services for businesses to thrive.
3. **Intelligent**: Utilizing cutting-edge technology and smart strategies.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Balanced Modern**: Clean, solid surfaces with intentional depth through subtle shadows, borders, and background color shifts. Minimal clarity (high legibility, intentional white space) with purposeful visual hierarchy.
- **Core Palette**: Modern Orange (`#f97316`), Amber (`#f59e0b`), and Stone-based neutrals.
- **Visual Language**: Solid card surfaces with defined borders, subtle shadow depth, and interactive feedback using Framer Motion.
- **Typography**: Inter (Body) and Plus Jakarta Sans (Display).

### Design Principles
1. **Mirroring Excellence**: Ensure a full RTL layout that goes beyond simple text alignment, maintaining an intuitive visual hierarchy (F-pattern) for both Arabic and English readers.
2. **Solid Depth**: Create depth and modernity through solid surfaces, defined borders, and subtle shadows (using `--surface-card-border` and `--shadow-surface-md`) — never through blur effects or transparency that compromise readability.
3. **Empowering Intelligence**: Design components that feel proactive and smart, using purposeful animations and gradients (like `--gradient-primary`) to guide the user rather than just decorate.
4. **Local Relevance**: Respect Arabic linguistic nuances and cultural context in icons, metaphors, and directional elements.
5. **Accessible by Design**: Prioritize high-contrast ratios using Stone neutrals (`--surface-900` for text on `--surface-50` backgrounds), keyboard navigability, and screen-reader support as fundamental requirements for every component.

## Design Tokens

### Core Colors
- **Primary**: `--primary` (`#f97316`) — Modern Orange. Used for CTAs, highlights, and primary brand elements.
- **Accents**: `--accent-amber` (`#f59e0b`), `--accent-cyan` (`#06b6d4`), and `--accent-emerald` (`#10b981`).
- **Surface**: Stone-based neutrals (e.g., `--surface-50` for backgrounds, `--surface-card-bg` for components).

### Depth & Elevation
- **Card Surfaces**: `.surface-card` (solid background, defined border, subtle shadow).
- **Shadows**: `.shadow-surface-sm` to `.shadow-surface-xl` using Slate-based translucency for depth.
- **Interactive**: `.surface-btn-primary` uses `--gradient-primary` and a distinctive glow shadow (`--shadow-button`).

### Gradients
- **Primary Gradient**: `--gradient-primary` (Orange to Amber @ 135deg).
- **Mesh Backgrounds**: `--gradient-mesh-1` through `--gradient-mesh-3` for subtle page depth.
- **Hero Surface**: `--gradient-hero` (soft tinted glow).
